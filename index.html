About Me

Prior to my technical career, I had been a college-level writing teacher, a professional fundraising consultant, and a not-for-profit development director and board member. I followed that with an eight year stint at IBM where I worked with mainframe computers and databases and helped managed projects incorporating web-based technologies. Today I combine these experiences as a website developer supporting specialized appeals and capital campaigns for a variety of non-profit clients.
